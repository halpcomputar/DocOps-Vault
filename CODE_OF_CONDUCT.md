# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive and specific.
- Respect privacy and operational security boundaries.
- Do not post private client, customer, employer, or personal data.
- Assume maintainers may choose not to accept a contribution even when it is well-intentioned.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Posting secrets, credentials, or private operational data.
- Repeated low-effort spam, off-topic promotion, or hostile demands.
- Trying to pressure maintainers into publishing private information or accepting unsafe workflows.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, delete, lock, or report issues, comments, pull requests, and discussions that violate this code.

Maintainers may block users who repeatedly violate these expectations.
